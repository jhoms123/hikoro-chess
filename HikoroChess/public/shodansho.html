How to Play Sho Dan Sho

Setup & Turn Structure

  • Player Count: 2-4 players.
  • First Turn: Each player places their Chysalith flower in any intersection within their designated starting gate.
  • Rest of Turns: Players must perform 2 move sets per turn:
    1. Move your Chysalith flower.
    2. Move or place any other flower.

General Rules

Movement

  • Pieces move orthogonally across lines, stopping on intersections.
  • Landing on an enemy piece captures it (unless uncapturable), stopping your piece.
  • Most pieces have set step counts. You move to an intersection, then can switch directions for the next step.
  • "In view of": On the same straight line with no blocking pieces between.

Dropping

  • Drop on your Starting Gate.
  • OR drop in a Field IF:
    • You have at least one piece in that field area.
    • Your Chysaliths in that field exceed any other player's.

Winning Conditions

Win by holding the corners of the Garden areas. At least one surrounding flower must be a Chysalith.

  • 2 Players: Central Garden + 2 smaller gardens.
  • 3 Players: Central Garden + 1 smaller garden.
  • 4 Players: Central Garden.
Chysalith Penalty: A Chysalith must always be on board. If lost, you must use your turn to place one from your hand. Lose both = Eliminated. In 3P/4P, if your closest garden is fully captured by an opponent, your Chysaliths are frozen until you regain control.

Piece Reference

Piece Count Movement & Abilities
★ Star Flower 4 Moves any number of intersections in one direction. Cannot cross rivers.
♣ Clover Flower 4 Moves 4 steps. When in view of a friendly Clover, it may jump to its position and pick a new direction (other than where it came from) to move any number of intersections. Can chain jumps through multiple Clovers (max 1 use per Clover).
✿ Daisy Flower 6 Moves 3 steps. Cannot cross rivers.
✥ Water Flower 6 Moves 2 steps. Can only be dropped in a green zone (field), and may not leave the zone it was dropped in.
☼ Sun Flower 3 Moves 4 steps. Uncapturable and cannot capture. Cannot have view of an opponent Chysalith; if targeted, must be moved or discarded. Can be picked up from the board to hand. Stops Shining flowers it is in view of.
✺ Shining Flower 3 Moves 3 steps. Chysaliths cannot move through lines giving view of this piece, nor drop/move into field zones where it is located. Freezes enemy Chysaliths already in its field. Paused if in view of a Sun Flower.
C Chysalith 2 Moves 2 steps. Crucial Piece. Cannot move into field zones with a Shining flower or move into/through a line that has a Shining flower in view.
